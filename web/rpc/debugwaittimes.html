debugwaittimes

A diagnostic method that collects wait-time and profiling information from the Database, the DigiByte Core connection, and the Chain Analyzer to help identify performance bottlenecks.

Result

NameTypeDescription
(result)array of stringsThe combined profiling report, split into one string per line. Each line is a piece of wait-time / profiling text gathered from the Database, DigiByte Core, and Chain Analyzer.

Examples

DigiAssetWindows-cli.exe debugwaittimes

curl --user myusername --data-binary '{"jsonrpc": "1.0", "id": "curltest", "method": "debugwaittimes", "params": []}' -H 'content-type: text/plain;' http://127.0.0.1:14024/
