How to call these methods

Every command has two forms — a short CLI call, and a raw curl JSON-RPC call. Both are shown in each method's Examples. There are two RPC interfaces:

DigiAsset Asset/IPFS methods — DigiAssetWindows-cli.exe <method>, RPC port 14024.
Core Blockchain, wallet & network methods — digibyte-cli <method>, RPC port 14022. Needs rpcuser/rpcpassword from digibyte.conf.

Pick a method on the left to see what it does, its arguments, and copy-paste examples.
